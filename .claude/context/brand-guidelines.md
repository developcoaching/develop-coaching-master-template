# Develop Coaching Brand Guidelines

## Logo Formats

The Develop Coaching logo features a ruler icon as the "L" in "DEVELOP".

### Variations
- **Default**: Stacked format with "DEVELOP" above "COACHING"
- **Banner**: Horizontal format with "DEVELOP COACHING" on one line

## Typography

**Primary Font**: Source Sans Pro

Used in both regular and bold weights for all brand communications.

## Design Principles

- Clean, professional interfaces
- Focus on clarity and ease of use
- Use accent colors sparingly for emphasis
- Maintain consistent spacing and typography
- Mobile-first responsive design
