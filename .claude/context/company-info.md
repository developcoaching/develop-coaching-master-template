# Develop Coaching - Company Information

## About

Develop Coaching provides business coaching and development tools specifically designed for construction business owners in the UK and Australia.

## Mission

Empowering construction business owners to build better businesses through structured coaching, goal tracking, and business development tools.

## Target Markets

- **United Kingdom**: Primary market
- **Australia**: Secondary market

## Target Audience

- Construction business owners
- Small to medium-sized construction companies
- Trade business owners (electricians, plumbers, builders, etc.)
- Construction managers looking to start their own businesses

## Key Services

- Business coaching and mentoring
- Goal tracking and accountability
- Business development tools and software
- Financial planning and management tools
- Performance tracking and analytics

## Technical Preferences

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React, TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (configured with brand colors)
- **Database**: Supabase or similar
- **Hosting**: Vercel or similar
- **Authentication**: Supabase Auth or similar

## Development Standards

- Mobile-first responsive design
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA minimum)
- Performance optimized (Core Web Vitals)
- SEO friendly
- Clean, maintainable code
